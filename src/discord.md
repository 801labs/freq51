## New Members & Onboarding

When you join the Freq51 Discord server, we guide you through a **simple onboarding process** to get set up and connected to your community.

1. **Select Your Region**  
   You will be prompted to select the region you live in (Utah or Idaho). This helps with local coordination and Weekly Net participation. Available regions:

   **Idaho**
   - Northern: Benewah, Bonner, Boundary, Clearwater, Idaho, Kootenai, Latah, Lewis, Nez Perce, Shoshone
   - Central: Adams, Butte, Clark, Custer, Fremont, Jefferson, Lemhi, Valley
   - South Western: Ada, Boise, Canyon, Elmore, Gem, Owyhee, Payette, Washington
   - South Central: Blaine, Camas, Cassia, Gooding, Jerome, Lincoln, Minidoka, Twin Falls
   - South Eastern: Bannock, Bear Lake, Bingham, Bonneville, Caribou, Franklin, Madison, Oneida, Power, Teton

   **Utah**
   - Northern: Cache, Davis, Morgan, Rich, Weber
   - Central: Salt Lake, Sanpete, Utah, Wasatch
   - Eastern: Carbon, Daggett, Duchesne, Emery, Grand, San Juan, Summit, Uintah, Wayne
   - Western: Box Elder, Juab, Millard, Tooele
   - Southern: Beaver, Garfield, Iron, Kane, Piute, Sevier, Washington

   **Other**
   - Just Visiting The Area: For those passing through Idaho or Utah

2. **Purpose Question**  
   You’ll be asked if you are here for **Meshtastic** or **MeshCore** participation. This helps route your notifications and relevant channels.

3. **Weekly Net Notifications**  
   You will have the option to opt in to receive reminders for the Weekly Net, including start-time notifications and follow-up summaries.

This process ensures everyone is set up for participation and can engage with the right region and mesh type.

## About Freq51

The Freq51 community is building an **open community mesh** that anyone can join.

This network is designed as a **radio-frequency communication tool** for:

- Emergency and disaster response
- Off-grid (non-internet) coordination
- Experimentation and hobbyist projects
- Learning and collaboration around mesh networking

**No radio license is required** to use Meshtastic, making it accessible to newcomers and experienced operators alike.

Whether you're here to experiment, support emergency preparedness, or explore mesh networking, you're welcome.

## Freq51 Discord Bot

The Discord bot supports the Weekly Net and general mesh activity by tracking node check-ins, ownership, and participation over time. It helps answer questions like:

- When did I check in?
- Which nodes are associated with me?
- How consistent has my participation been?
- How do I compare on the leaderboard?

## Available Bot Commands

## MeshDrone Commands:
- **~ant** – Freq51 Recommended Antennas
- **~config** – Freq51 Config Tool
- **~cov** – Freq51 Coverage Map
- **~help** – Provides template for support
- **~hopwatch** – Mesh hop channels
- **~infra** – Freq51 Infrastructure
- **~fq51** – Freq51 Website
- **~regions** – Discord Regions map
- **~migrate** – Freq51 Migrate page
- **~mv** – Freq51 Meshview site
- **~on** – Freq51 On-Boarding link
- **~loss** –  Coax Line loss
- **~ma** – Freq51 Malla site
- **~mqtt** – Freq51 MQTT sites.
- **~pihat** – Links to Pi-Hat hardware

## Role Config Guides:
- **~client** – Freq51 Client Role Settings
- **~mute** – Freq51 Client Mute Role Settings
- **~hide** – Freq51 Client Hidden Role Settings
- **~router** – Freq51 Router Role Settings
- **~late** – Freq51 Router Late Role Settings

## Weekly-Net Commands:
- **/mycheckins** – See your recent check-ins! Only shows nodes assigned to your Discord username.
- **/usernodes** – Get a list of all nodes assigned to you.
- **/checkinsbydate** – Lists all Weekly-Net check-ins by node for a specific date. Shows each node that reported in on that date, along with time and owner.
- **/claimnode** - Allows you to self assign/claim a node owned by you.
- **/mystreaks** - Shows your checkin streaks
- **/streakleaderboard**  - Shows the current leaderboard of checkins.

## HWT Commands
- **/hwt** - Will give a link you can check RF based on the lat/lon cords provided.
